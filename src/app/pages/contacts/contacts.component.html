Studio name
CU/ONE brings together two symbolic origins: CU stands for copper – the first metal shaped by human hands, symbolizing craftsmanship, creative foundations, and reflecting the Latin origin of the name Kipras. This also inspired the copper-toned orange used in the identity. ONE represents the first spark of creation. Together, they express the studio’s link to ancient craft, personal roots, and new creative beginnings.
